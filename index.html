Research Interests

Topics I'm currently exploring and writing about.

LLM Applications

Experimenting with large language models, prompt engineering, and building practical applications that leverage modern AI capabilities.

AI Safety & Security

Researching prompt injection attacks, guardrails, and moderation systems. Understanding how to build AI systems that fail gracefully.

RAG & Vector Search

Exploring retrieval-augmented generation, embedding models, chunking strategies, and how to build effective knowledge retrieval systems.

MLOps & Evaluation

Studying how to evaluate AI systems, build reproducible pipelines, and measure what actually matters in model performance.

AI Agents

Building multi-agent collaboration systems like Virtual TeamWork, exploring how AI agents can work together autonomously on complex tasks.

Steerability & SAEs

Researching sparse autoencoders and interpretability techniques to understand and steer model behavior at the feature level.
